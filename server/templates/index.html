Monsters Ink

Monsters Ink is a new-aged take on an old card game where players draw fierce monsters and pitch them against their opponents.

Gameplay

  • Draw your own card
  • Give it unique abilities
  • Challenge your foes and let the lobby decide your fate.
  • Discuss which opponent wins in text chat.
  • Winner gets to add an ability to the card.

AWB Team

Monsters Ink is a card game brought to life by Artifical Worm Blast, a Spring 2019 Software Design and Documentation team from RPI.
